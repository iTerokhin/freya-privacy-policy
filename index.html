  • All your app data is stored only on your device. Deleting the App removes this data from the device. If you delete the App, all information stored in the App on that device — including your user information and your local report history — will be deleted and cannot be recovered, reconstructed, or otherwise restored by us.

Deleting the App from your device does not automatically delete all data stored by third-party providers.
